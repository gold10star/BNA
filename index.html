ATM/BNA AUTO RECONCILIATION APP

ATM/BNA Auto Reconciliation

Step 1 — Upload

Take a clear photo of the Host Total Receipt from the ATM or BNA machine and upload it. The app automatically detects the machine type.

Step 2 — Loading Details

Enter the number of notes loaded into each cassette (₹100, ₹500, ₹200). All three values are mandatory — enter 0 if a cassette was not loaded.

Step 3 — Brought Back

Enter the number of notes physically brought back for each denomination (₹100, ₹500, ₹200). Excess and Short are calculated automatically. Physical Balance updates in real time.

Step 4 — Switch & GL Balances

Enter the Switch Balance and GL Balance, then click Calculate Differences. Results show as Tallied, Excess, or Short.

Step 5 — Save, Email & History

After calculating differences, Sign in with Google to save the record. Use ✉ Email to send the report. Tap 📋 History to view all saved records and 📊 Export to download as Excel.
