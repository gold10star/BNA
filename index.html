ATM/BNA AUTO RECONCILIATION APP

ATM/BNA Auto Reconciliation

Step 1 — Upload

Take a clear photo of the Host Total Receipt from the ATM/BNA machine and upload it. The app auto-detects whether it is BNA or ATM.

Step 2 — Loading Details

After scanning, a popup asks for notes loaded into each cassette. Enter the counts or tap Skip.

Step 3 — Edit & Recalculate

Tap any value in the table to correct it. Remaining auto-adjusts as you type. Tap Recalculate to refresh totals. For BNA, if there is any excess, edit the Excess column directly in the table.

Step 4 — Switch & GL Balances

Enter Switch and GL balances to see Physical vs GL and Physical vs Switch differences with traffic light indicators.

Step 5 — Print

Tap Print on top of the table and save as PDF for future records
